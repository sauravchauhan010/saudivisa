1-Month Single-Entry Saudi Business and Tourist Visa

Saudi Arabia has quickly become a popular travel destination, attracting visitors eager to explore its rich history and vibrant culture. By the end of 2023, the country welcomed an estimated 93.8 million tourists, marking significant growth from previous years. But how do you travel to Saudi Arabia for business or leisure? The first step is securing a Saudi Arabia visa.

What is a Single-Entry Saudi Visa?

A single-entry Saudi visa grants travelers the right to enter the country once within a specified validity period. It is typically issued for short-term purposes such as business trips, tourism, or family visits. This visa, provided by Visit Saudi, remains valid for 30 days from the date of issuance.

It's important to note that multiple entries are not allowed under this visa. It is issued when the travel plan indicates that a one-time visit is sufficient for the intended purpose.

Saudi Arabia is emerging as a major tourist hub with centuries-old historical sites and cultural landmarks. Additionally, it offers excellent business opportunities for global investors. With a single-entry Saudi business visa, you can attend meetings, explore investment opportunities, and engage in business collaborations.

However, professionals such as doctors, engineers, and teachers are not eligible for a business visa and must apply for long-term work or study permits instead.

Documents Required for a Single-Entry Saudi Arabia Visit Visa

  • Visa Application Form: A completed form with accurate travel details.
  • Valid Passport: A scanned copy of a passport valid for at least six months, with two blank pages.
  • Recent Photographs : Two passport-sized photos (2x2 inches) as per Saudi visa specifications.
  • Travel Itinerary & Flight Tickets Proof of travel plans, including return flight details.
  • Accommodation Confirmation : Hotel booking or an official invitation letter.
  • Financial Proof: Documents verifying sufficient funds to support your stay.
  • Travel Insurance: A policy covering medical emergencies.
  • Proof of Nationality: Documents showing ties to your home country, such as family, employment, or property ownership.

Additional Documents for a Single-Entry Saudi Business Visa

For a business visa, the following extra documents are required:

  • Invitation Letter : A document issued by a registered Saudi company.
  • Authorization Letter: A notarized letter confirming your role in business activities.
  • Company Cover Letter: A formal letter from your employer outlining the purpose of the trip, position, and stay duration.
  • Business Registration Certificate: Proof of your company’s legal status.
  • Past Business Dealings Documents (contracts, invoices, etc.) proving previous engagements with Saudi firms, if applicable.

Saudi Arabia Single-Entry Visa Validity

A 1-month single-entry Saudi visa is valid for 30 days from the date of issuance. Travelers can stay for up to 30 days from their date of arrival.

Extending a Single-Entry Saudi Visa

If you need to extend your stay in Saudi Arabia, it is possible under certain conditions. A family visit visa can be extended up to eight times, with each extension allowing an additional 30-day stay. This means you can legally stay for up to 270 days with proper extensions.

To avoid fines or penalties, visa extensions must be requested at least 7 days before expiration. Overstaying beyond the approved period without renewal may result in legal consequences.
