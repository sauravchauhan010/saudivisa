Multiple Entry Business and Visit Visa for Saudi Arabia

MIf you're planning frequent trips to Saudi Arabia from the UAE, securing a multiple-entry visa can simplify your travel experience. Whether for business, family visits, or tourism, this visa allows you to enter and exit the Kingdom multiple times within a year.

With Saudi Arabia emerging as a major business and tourism hub, obtaining a visa has never been easier. VisitSaudi ensures a streamlined process, helping UAE residents apply for their Saudi multiple-entry visa with ease.

What is a Multiple-Entry Saudi Visa?

Key Features:

  • Validity: 1 year (365 days) from the issue date
  • Stay Duration: Up to 90 days per visit
  • Extensions:Possible for up to 180 days under specific conditions

Depending on your reason for travel, you can apply for:

  • Visit Visa: Ideal for tourism, family visits, cultural activities, and special events.
  • Business Visa: Suitable for business meetings, corporate events, trade exhibitions, and investment-related activities.

Required Documents for Saudi Arabia Multiple-Entry Visa

Applicants must provide the following:

  • Visa Application Form: Filled with accurate details.
  • Valid Passport: Minimum validity of six months with at least two blank pages.
  • Recent Photographs : Two recent photos with a white background.
  • Financial Proof: Bank statements or salary slips confirming financial stability.
  • Family Visit Proof: If applicable, documents proving your relationship with the invitee.
  • Health Insurance:Travel medical insurance approved by Saudi authorities.
  • Proof of Home Country Ties:Employment verification, business ownership, or student enrollment to demonstrate intent to return.
  • Guardian Consent Letter: If a minor (under 18) is traveling, a notarized consent letter from a parent or guardian is required.

Additional Documents for Business Visa Applicants

If you're traveling for business purposes, the following will be required:

  • Business Invitation Letter: A letter from a Saudi-based company confirming the purpose of the visit.
  • Company Registration Documents: Official proof of the Saudi company’s registration.
  • Employer Cover Letter: A letter from your employer verifying your job position and reason for travel.
  • Business Relationship Proof:If applicable, documents showing past business dealings with the Saudi company.

Processing Time for Saudi Multiple-Entry Visa

The average processing time is 5-7 working days, depending on application details, nationality, and travel history. Please note that Saudi public holidays and weekends do not count in the processing period.
