Saudi Arabia Visa Requirements for UAE Residents – Visit Saudi

Applying for a Saudi Arabia visa from Dubai comes with certain document requirements, depending on the visa type. To simplify the process, here is a checklist of essential documents you need to prepare.

Common Saudi Arabia Visa Documents

1. Visa Application Form

  • The visa process is incomplete without a duly completed application form.
  • Ensure all details, including personal information and supporting documents, are accurately filled in.

2. Passport

  • The passport must be valid for a minimum of six months from the arrival date in Saudi Arabia.
  • It should be in good condition, with at least two blank pages for visa stamps.

3. Passport-Sized Photograph

  • A newly captured passport-sized photograph with a plain white background.
  • Saudi visa photo size: 45 mm x 35 mm.
  • The face should cover 80% of the image, with a neutral expression (no sunglasses or hats).
  • Two copies of the photograph must be submitted.

4. UAE Residence Visa

  • A valid UAE residence visa with at least three months’ validity from the intended entry date into Saudi Arabia.

5. Flight Itinerary

  • A copy of your round-trip flight booking is required.
  • Do not book tickets until your visa is issued to avoid unnecessary expenses.

6. Financial Proof

  • Evidence of financial capability to cover trip expenses.
  • A recent bank statement or other financial documents must be provided.

7. No Objection Certificate (NOC)

A NOC letter from your employer stating:

  • Your salary, designation, and date of employment commencement.
  • The purpose of travel to Saudi Arabia.
  • The letter must be directed to the Saudi Embassy.

8. Travel Insurance

  • Travel insurance from a recognized company is mandatory.
  • Some cases may require a health or medical certificate.

8. Travel Insurance

  • Travel insurance from a recognized company is mandatory.
  • Some cases may require a health or medical certificate.

9. Proof of Accommodation

  • A hotel booking confirmation or accommodation details.

Additional Document Requirements Based on Visa Type

Saudi Arabia Business Visa

  • An invitation letter from a Saudi company (electronic format accepted), stating the purpose, duration, and nature of the visit.
  • A letter from your employer mentioning the business purpose and duration of travel.
  • Company registration certificates of both the UAE-based company and the Saudi company.

Saudi Arabia Visit Visa

  • A sponsor’s invitation letter from Saudi Arabia.
  • Proof of relationship with the sponsor.

Saudi Arabia Multiple-Entry Visa

  • Legal UAE residency proof.
  • For a business visa, a business invitation letter from a Saudi-based company is necessary.
  • For a visit visa, supporting documentation related to the visit purpose must be provided.

Saudi Arabia Student Visa

  • icial letter of acceptance from a recognized Saudi Arabian university.

Documents for Minors (Under 18)

  • Birth certificate for proof of age and identity.
  • Proof of relationship with the accompanying adult (family registration document).
  • Parental authorization letter from a legal guardian, allowing the minor to travel.

Final Notes

Always check with the Saudi Embassy or consulate for the latest visa requirements, as regulations may change. You can apply online through the official portal or consult a local visa agency for a smooth application process.
